Key Ingredients

Mango Oil

Lightweight and nutrient-rich, mango oil helps soften the lips while supporting natural moisture balance.

Benefit: Nourishing Origin: Cambodia

Coconut Oil

Smooth and comforting, coconut oil helps reduce dryness and leaves lips feeling supple.

Benefit: Softening Origin: Cambodia

Beewax

Naturally protective, beeswax forms a gentle barrier that seals in moisture and shields lips from dryness.

Benefit: Protecting Origin: Cambodia

Vitamin E

Known for its antioxidant properties, vitamin E helps support lip health and maintain a smooth appearance.

Benefit: Restoring Origin: Cambodia

Lily Essential Oil

Lightly infused for a delicate scent, lily essential oil adds a calm, refined aromatic touch.

Benefit: Fragrance Origin: Cambodia
